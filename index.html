How it works

  1. Donors submit unexpired, sealed medicines with details.
  2. Receivers request needed medicines with contact info.
  3. Admins or volunteers review submissions and coordinate matches.

About Medi For U

Medi For U is a community-driven initiative to reduce medicine waste and improve access for those in need. We provide a simple way for donors to offer safe-to-donate, unexpired medicines and for receivers to request what they need.

Safety first: Only donate medicines that are sealed, unexpired, in original packaging, and legally transferable in your region. We recommend consulting healthcare professionals before using any donated medicine.

Privacy: Contact information is used solely for coordination between donors and receivers. This demo stores data locally in your browser and does not upload to a server.

Get involved: Donate on the Donor page or request help on the Receiver page. Have questions or want to volunteer? Reach out to us.
